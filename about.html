About Leni

Made by hand. Made to last. Made for real people.

Where it started

Why Leni exists

Leni was born out of frustration with fast fashion — with clothes that fall apart, that look like everyone else's, that don't feel like anything. The idea was simple: what if you could just have something made properly?

Every piece is designed to be worn constantly. We use natural fabrics — organic cotton, linen, cotton canvas — that soften and improve with age. The cuts are relaxed and unfussy. There are no logos, no trends, no gimmicks.

We make everything in small batches and take pre-orders so nothing goes to waste. It's slower, but it feels right.

What we stand for

How we work

Small batches

We make limited runs so everything sells and nothing goes to landfill. No overproduction, ever.

Made by hand

Every seam is sewn by us. We take our time because we want each piece to be something you'll keep.

Natural fabrics

Organic cotton, linen, and canvas. Materials that breathe, move, and age beautifully.

Real people

We make clothes for actual bodies in a range of sizes. Fit matters as much as fabric.

The process

From fabric to finished piece

We source our fabrics in small quantities from UK and European mills. Once the fabric arrives, we cut, press, and sew each piece ourselves. It takes time — usually a day or two per batch — but we think that's part of what makes it worthwhile.

Pre-orders are fulfilled in the order they're placed. You'll receive an email when your piece is ready to ship, along with a tracking number.
