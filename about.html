Made by hand.
Made to last.

Leni started from a simple idea: make fewer things, but make them properly. Every piece is sewn by hand using quality natural fabrics. We don't do mass production — we make what we need, when we need it.

The process

Cut, sewn, and finished by hand.

We source fabrics from specialist mills that share our values — natural fibres, responsible production, quality that ages well.

Every garment goes through the same process: it's cut by hand on a large table, sewn on domestic machines, and pressed and folded before it leaves our studio.

Our values

Small runs.
No waste.

We keep our runs small so nothing goes to waste, and every order gets the attention it deserves.

When you order from Leni, you're getting something made specifically for you — not pulled off a rail somewhere in a warehouse.
