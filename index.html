The story

Clothes that are
made to last.

Leni started from a simple idea: make fewer things, but make them properly. Every piece is sewn by hand using quality natural fabrics. We don't do mass production — we make what we need, when we need it.

We keep our runs small so nothing goes to waste, and every order gets the attention it deserves. When you order from Leni, you're getting something made specifically for you.
